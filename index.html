About Us

The Leo Club of St. Joseph's College, Anuradhapura, chartered on July 24, 2025, is the first in the school's history and one of the most energetic in Leo District 306 D11, Sri Lanka. Starting with 20 members, the club now has 35 active Alpha Leos committed to leadership and community service.

Backed by its sponsoring Lions Club, it empowers young Josephians to create meaningful change. Guided by the motto "Unconquered Spirit, Limitless Possibilities," the club continues its promising journey of service and inspiration.

Our club may be new, but our spirit is powerful — and our journey of service has only just begun.
