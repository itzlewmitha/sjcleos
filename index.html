OUR MISSION

The Leo Club of St. Joseph's College, Anuradhapura, chartered on July 24, 2025, represents the first chapter in our school's history and stands as one of the most dynamic forces in Leo District 306 D11, Sri Lanka.

Beginning with 20 visionary members, our legion has expanded to 35 Alpha Leos—each committed to leadership, innovation, and galactic-scale community service.

Empowered by our sponsoring Lions Club, we equip young Josephians to engineer meaningful change across our universe. Guided by our motto "Unconquered Spirit, Limitless Possibilities", we navigate a promising trajectory of service, leadership, and inspiration.

Though our chapter is new, our spirit is ancient and our resolve is absolute. Our journey through the stars of service has just ignited.
